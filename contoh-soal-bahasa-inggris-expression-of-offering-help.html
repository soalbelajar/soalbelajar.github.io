2. expression of offering help note : jelaskan, cara penggunaan, contoh, dan dialog. ke dalam bhs Inggris​


Penjelasan:

Expression of Offering Help (Menawarkan Bantuan)

1. Explanation:

Offering help is a polite and thoughtful gesture to show your willingness to assist someone in a certain situation. It conveys kindness and empathy, and it's commonly used in daily conversations, both formal and informal settings.

2. How to Use:

To offer help, you can use various phrases and expressions. It's essential to maintain a friendly and sincere tone while making the offer. Here are some common expressions:

a. "Can I help you with anything?"

b. "Do you need any assistance?"

c. "Is there anything I can do for you?"

d. "Would you like me to lend a hand?"

e. "If you need any help, feel free to ask."

f. "Let me know if there's anything I can do to help."

g. "Need a hand with that?"

3. Examples:

a. "Can I help you with anything? You seem a bit lost."

b. "Do you need any assistance carrying those heavy bags?"

c. "Is there anything I can do for you? You can count on me."

d. "Would you like me to lend a hand with the preparation?"

e. "If you need any help organizing the event, just let me know."

f. "Let me know if there's anything I can do to help you through this tough time."

g. "Need a hand with setting up the equipment?"

4. Dialog:

Formal Setting:

A: Excuse me, sir. I noticed you're struggling with the projector setup. Can I help you with anything?

B: Oh, thank you. That would be great. I can't seem to get it working properly.

Informal Setting:

A: Hey, I heard you're moving to a new apartment this weekend. Do you need any assistance with packing?

B: Thanks for asking! Yeah, I could use some help with the boxes.

Remember, offering help is a kind gesture, but it's essential to respect the other person's decision if they decline the offer. Always be sincere and genuine in your willingness to assist others.

13. What is the aim of offering help expression?


Jawaban:

The aim of the expression of offering help is to to offer service/help to others.

=> Tujuan dari ungkapan menawarkan bantuan adalah untuk menawarkan layanan / bantuan kepada orang lain.

PEMBAHASAN:

Asking and offering for help adalah salah satu expression dalam bahasa inggris yang menyatakan bahwa kita meminta dan memberikan bantuan kepada seseorang. Biasanya kalimat asking/offering for help dimulai dengan kata Modal Auxiliaries yang berupa can, could, will, would, shall, should, dan may.

Patterns of offering help/service are:

May + I + verb 1?

Would you like me to + verb 1?

Can + I + verb 1?

Do you want me + to + verb 1?

Contoh Kalimat

Offering for Help

May I help you? (bolehkah aku membantumu?)

Would you like a cup of coffee?(maukah kamu secangkir kopi?)

May I give you a hand? (Bisakah aku membantumu?)

Shall I bring you a pillow? (haruskah aku membawakanmu bantal?)

Can I do the dishes for you? (bisakah aku mencuci piring untukmu?)

Shall I help you with your project? (haruskah aku menolongmu dengan tugasmu?)

Would you care for another cup of tea? (maukah kamu secangkir teh lagi?)

Accepting Offers (Menerima Bantuan)

Thank you. (terimakasih)

Yes, please. (ya tolong)

I’d like it very much. (aku akan sangat menyukainya)

Yes please. I really appreciate it. (ya tolong, aku sangat menghargainya)

Thank you, it’s very kind of you (terimakasih, kamu baik sekali)

Yes, please, that would be lovely(ya tolong, itu akan sangat menyenangkan)

Declining Offers (Menolak Bantuan)

No thanks. (tidak terimakasih)

I can’t, thanks anyway (aku tidak bisa, terimakasih)

No, I really won’t, thank you (tidak, aku tidak mau, terimakasih)

It’s okay, I can do it myself. (terimakasih, aku bisa melakukannya sendiri)

No thank you. (tidak terimakasih)

No thanks, I don’t need any help (tidak terimakasih, aku tidak membutuhkan bantuan)

Don’t worry, I will do it myself. (jangan khawatir, aku akan melakukannya sendiri)

That’s alright, I will manage it on my own. (tidak apa apa, aku akan melakukannya sendiri)
