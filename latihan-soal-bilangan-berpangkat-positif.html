2. SOAL LATIHAN1. Ubahlah bilangan berpangkat negatif berikut merjadi bilangan berpangkat positif!a.p^-5b.1/2^-3c.8/5^b-2 ​


a. p^(-5)

= (p^(-1))^5

= (1/p)^5

= 1/p^5

b. 1/2^(-3)

= (1/2^(-1))³

= 2³

c. 8/5^(b-2)

= 8/((5^b)/(5²))

= 8. (5²)/(5^b)

= 8. 25/5^b

= 200/5^b
