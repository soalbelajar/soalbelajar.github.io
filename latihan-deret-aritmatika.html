b = 14 - 5 = 9

Ditanya :

S16 = ...?

Jawab :

[tex]Sn = \frac{n}{2} (2a + (n - 1)b) \\ S16 = \frac{16}{2} (2 \times 5 + (16 - 1) \times 9) \\ S16 = 8(10 + (15 \times 9)) \\ S16 = 8(10 + 135) \\ S16 = 8(145) \\ S16 = 1160[/tex]
