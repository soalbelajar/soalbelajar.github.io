34. they like vegetables?​


Jawaban :

...they like vegetables?

→ Do they like vegetables?

Penjelasan:

Kata untuk mengisi kalimat rumpang di atas adalah "do" karena kalimat tersebut merupakan kalimat simple present tense interrogative.Berikut penjelasan mengenai simple present tense interrogative :

Rumus/formula :

Kalimat interrogative/kalimat tanya (?)

→ Do/does + S + verb-1 + O + ?

Contoh :

Do they like vegetables?Does she write a letter?Does my father buy a new car?Do we study english?

Note :

Do → untuk subject " I, they, we, you"

Does → untik subject "he, she, it"
