Questions 1-10 refer to the following passage.

The most familiar speleothems (from the Greek word spelaion for the cave and thema for deposit), the decorative dripstone features found in caves, are stalactites and stalagmites. Stalactites hang downward from the ceiling of thecave and are formed as drop after drop of water slowly trickles through crack on thecave roof. Stalagmites grow upward from the floor of the cave, generally as a result of water dripping from an overhead stalactite. A column forms when a stalactite and a stalagmite grow until they join. A “curtain” or “drapery” begins to form on an inclined ceiling when drops of water trickle along a slope.

Natural openings on the surface that lead to caves are called sinkholes. or swallow holes. Streams sometimes disappear down these holes and flow through the cavern. Rivers may flow from one mountain to another through a series of caves . Some caverns have sinkholes in their floors. Water often builds up a rim of dripstone around the edge of the hole. Dripping water often contains dissolved minerals as well as acid. These minerals too will be deposited; and they may give rich coloring to the deposits. If minerals in the water change, layers of different colors may be formed.

1.Stalagmites are formed by …

(A) drops of water which enter throughcracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

2. Sinkholes are …

(A) the decorative dripstone features found in caves.

(B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(C) colorful layers of mineral deposits.

(D) None of the above

Jawaban : (B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Sinkholes

(D) Curtains

(A) is straight.

(B) is crooked.

(C) is slanted.

(D) is wet.

Jawaban : (C) is slanted.

(A) Stalactites

(B) Stalagmites

(C) Slopes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (C) Slopes

(A) Geography

Jawaban : (D) Geology

7. “ Curtains” can also be called …

(A) column.

(B) draperies.

Jawaban : (B) draperies.

Legend of Origin Banyuwangi

Narrated, on the East coast of Java Island, there is a king named Praku Menak Prakosa. He had a handsome and mighty boy named Raden Banterang. However, his temper tantrums make his people a little hot.

One day Raden Banterang hunted into the forest. He meets a beautiful girl. He addressed the young woman, "Are you a goddess? Why is no one with you?"
The girl replied, "It's not a goddess, I'm a human, I'm running from the enemy's pursuit of royalty."

Raden Banterang gasped. Without showing his surprise, he asked, "What is in front of me now is the daughter of King Klungkung?". “That's right, my name is Surati." Next Raden Banterang took him to the palace. Finally, he married King Klungkung's daughter. One day, Princess Surati meets her own sister.

“Surati! Why are you marrying the man who killed your father? You must help me to destroy him!". “Adinda apologies, it is unlikely that Adinda killed her own husband." Hearing his brother's answer, his brother looked disappointed and left Surati.

One day Raden Banterang was hunting. While chasing a deer, a beggar approached him and said, "My lord Raden Banterang, my lord is threatened by the danger of death that your empress's consort." Having said that, the beggar disappeared.

Raden Banterang returned to the palace at once. The angry nature of Raden Banterang re-emerged. Then he invited Princess Surati to the estuary of a river. Told all the words of the beggar while he was in the woods. Raden Banterang was so angry, and said, "Is this your reward for what I did to you!" Quaking shocked, but she understands that her husband is being incited with sedition. He replied courteously, "Kakanda, Adinda did meet my brother, he asked Adinda to help revenge the death of our father, but Adinda refused, because Adinda loves Kakanda."

Hearing explanations of his wife, Raden Banterang still not believe. Then, he pulled a dagger at his waist and directed it to his wife. Seeing that, Surati said, "For the sincerity of Adinda's love, Adinda's life will be sacrificed to prove that Adinda does not betray Kakanda." Goodbye Kakanda! "
After saying so, Surati jumps into the river and disappears. Not long ago, smelled fragrant from the river. Raden Banterang cried while holding his body almost swayed. His heart was shaken by the departure of his wife.

Disebabkan karena, bahasa Inggris sendiri sudah dianggap dan diketahui cara penggunaan kalimat, grammar, dan juga sebagai sarana dalam memudahkan orang luar berinteraksi dengan orang-orang baru. Faktor penyebabnya adalah:

Penggunaan logat bahasa yang mudah.Mudah untuk dipahami atau digunakan dalam interaksi sehari-hari.Telah diakui oleh setiap Negara bahkan negara Asia Tenggara.Bahasa yang menarik dan mendunia, biasa dipakai dalam pengucapan anak muda sehari-hari.

Pembahasan

Terkenalnya bahasa Inggris dari awal-mula cerita sejarahnya:

Awalnya bahasa yang mendunia ketika zaman kuno ialah bahasa Perancis dan juga bahasa Eropa.

Namun, penggunaan sistem bahasa ini tidak banyak digunakan oleh masyarakat luas pada saat itu. Ditambah lagi karena kebingungan antara 2 bahasa yang harus mana digunakan. Kala itu, para ahli mencari sumber bahasa yang akan digunakan khusus sebagai sarana dalam memudahkan komunikasi antar orang yang berbeda bahasa dan negara. Diselidiki mulai dari penjajahan, perang Inggris dan juga mengkaji bahasanya.

Sampai pada akhirnya semua orang dan semua masyarakat memilih bahasa yang satu untuk sarana berinteraksi yakni, menggunakan bahasa Inggris.
