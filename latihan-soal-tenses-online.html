6. apakah soalnya itu sudah sesuai dengan tensesnya? jikalau belum bisa di perbaiki,, dan itu di ganti dengan kata SHE dan WE​


Jawaban:

1. (+) I'm busy.

( - ) She's not busy.

(?) Are we busy?

2. (+) I watch movie every night.

( - ) She doesn't watch movie every night.

(?) Do we watch movie every night?

3. (+) I'm learning Dutch.

( - ) She isn't learning Dutch.

(?) Are we learning Dutch?

4. (+) I've had a letter from Jerry.

(-) She's not had a letter from Jerry.

(?) Have we had a letter from Jerry?

5. (+) I was busy this morning.

(-) She wasn't busy this morning.

(?) Were we busy this morning?

6. (+) I sent the letter to my cousin.

(-) She didn't send the letter to my/ her cousin.

(?) Did we send the letter to my/our cousin?

7. (+) I was driving my car on my work.

(-) She wasn't driving my/her car on my/her work.

(?) Were we driving my/our car on my/our work?

8. (+) I had performed my violin skill.

(-) She hadn't performed her violin skill.

(?) Had we performed our violin skill?

9. (+) I will buy the newspaper.

(-) She won't buy the newspaper.

(?) Will we buy the newspaper?

10. (+) I will be driving to work.

(-) She won't be driving to work.

(?) Will we be driving to work?

11. (+) I would be hiking to the hill.

(-) She wouldn't be hiking to the hill.

(?) Will we be hiking the hill?

12. (+) I would go there.

(-) She wouldn't go there.

(?) Will we go there?

Note:

I'm = I am

I've = I have

won't = will not

wasn't = was not

weren't = were not

hadn't = had not

wouldn't = would not

didn't = did not

...'s = is / has

* ...'d = had / would

13. soal perfect tenseshelp​


Jawaban:

1. Buatlah kalimat future perfect tense menggunakan kata di dalam kurung.

a. Super Junior is creating a new song. They will have released their new song next month.

b. Panji has planned for his holiday. He will have gone to Bali next December.

2. Ubahlah kalimat berikut menjadi past perfect tense.

a. After the rain had stopped, they went to the beach.

b. When the bomb exploded, they had left the spot.

3. Terjemahkan kalimat berikut menggunakan present perfect tense.

a. Have Eka and Rika sung their favorite song at the party?

b. This is not the first time Sodikin has ridden a motorcycle

Penjelasan:

⁕ Future perfect tense, digunakan ketika menunjukkan suatu aksi yang telah selesai di masa depan. Polanya adalah:

(+) S + Will + Have + V3

(-) S + Will + not + Have + V3

(?) Will + S + Have + V3 + ...?

⁕ Past perfect tense, digunakan ketika menunjukkan suatu aksi yang telah selesai sebelum aksi lainnya terjadi di masa lalu.

(+) S + Had + V3

(-) S + Had + not + V3

(?) Had + S +  V3 + ...?

⁕ Present perfect tense, digunakan ketika menunjukkan suatu aksi yang dimulai di masa lalu dan tetap berpengaruh hingga sekarang.

(+) S + Have/Has + V3

(-) S + Have/Has + not + V3

(?) Have/Has + S +  V3 + ...?


14. Apa perbedaan dari masing-masing tenses? Tolong jelaskan dengan bahasa kalangan rendah /? Soalnya saya ngga pernah ngerti masalah tenses T_T


بسم الله الرّحمن الرحيم

1. Simple Present tense: subjek+kata kerja1(read,walk,see,eat)+keterangan/pelengkap(bisa brupa k.benda,k.tempat,k.waktu)ex: I eat Bakso.
2. Simple past: subjek+kata kerja2+keterangan/pelengkap *(jadi bdanya dgn yg pertama cuma di k.kerjanya aja. intinya kalo dia verb2(kkerja2) mka dia past tense. Ex: I went (k.krja ke-2 dr kata 'Go') to school yesterday.
3. Future tense: subjek+will/shall+k.kerja+ket./plengkap. Ex: I will meet you tomorrow.

4. Continuous tense: subjek+auxiliary verb(tobe: am,are,is)+ k.kerja1ditambah '-ing'+ ket./plengkap. Ex: I am reading Al-Qur'an. *(jadi continuous itu kalimat "sedang" jd cntoh tsb artinya sy sedang mmbca Al-Qur'an". kalo nemuin yg pake auxiliary (entah am,are,is) diikuti k.kerja pake '-ing' brarti itu tenses ini.
5. Present Perfect Tense: subjek+ auxiliary (have,has) + k.kerja3 +ket./plengkap. Ex: I have told you. *( jenis tense ini kata have/has berarti 'telah', jd artinya, "Saya telah memberitahumu". kata have/has ini harus diikuti dgn kata kerja ke-3, untuk menunjukkan bahwa di adalah perfect tense. 18. tuliskan rumus tenses,simple present tenses,simple past tenses,simple future tenses,beri masing 5 contoh ( +,-,? )​


Jawaban:

Rumus simple present tense yaitu

(+) subject + verb 1

(-) Subject + do/does + not + verb 1

(?) Do/does + subject + verb 1?

Rumus simple past tense yaitu

(+) subject + verb 2

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1

(?) Did + subject + verb 1?

Rumus simple future tense yaitu

(+) subject + will + verb 1

(-) Subject + will + not + verb 1

(?) Will+ subject + verb 1?

Penjelasan:

Contoh kalimat simple present tense yaitu

(+) I drink a glass of milk everyday.

(-) I don't drink a glass of milk everyday.

(?) Do I drink a glass of milk everyday?

Contoh kalimat simple past tense yaitu

(+) I slept on the sofa last night.

(-) I didn't sleep on the sofa last night.

(?) Did I sleep on the sofa last night?

Contoh kalimat simple future tense yaitu

(+) She will come to my house tomorrow.

(-) She will not come to my house tomorrow.

(?) Will she come to my house tomorrow?

19. Tau soal Bahasa Inggris soal tenses


Kata kerja yang menunjukan soal waktu, dibagi jadi 3:
1. Present tanse (sekarang)
2. Past tanse (lampau)
3. Future tanse (masa yang akan datang)Ada 16 tenses, look at below! 

PRESENT 
1. Simple Present Tense (waktu sekarang/biasa) 
2. Present Continous Tense (waktu berlangsung sekarang) 
3. Present Perfect Tense (waktu selesai sekarang) 
4. Present Perfect Continous (waktu selesai sedang berlangsung) 

PAST TENSE 
1. Simple Past Tense (waktu lampau) 
2. Past Continous Tense (waktu berlangsung lampau) 
3. Past Perfect Tense (waktu selesai lampau) 
4. Past Perfect Continous Tense (waktu sedang berlangsung selesai lampau) 

FUTURE TENSE 
1. Simple Future Tense (waktu yang akan datang) 
2. Future Continous Tense (waktu sedang berlangsung akan datang)
3. Future Perfect Tense (waktu selesai akan datang) 
4. Future Perfect Tense (waktu sedang berlangsung selesai akan datang) 

PAST FUTURE 
1. Past Future Tense (waktu yang akan datang pada waktu lampau)
2. Past Fuuture Continous Tense (waktu yang akan sedang terjadi pada waktu lampau) 
3. Past Future Perfect Tense (waktu yang akan selesai pada waktu lampau)  
4. Past Future Perfect Continous Tense (waktu yang akan sudah sedang berlangsung pada waktu lampau)

• (-) S + have/has + not + V3

Fungsi:
