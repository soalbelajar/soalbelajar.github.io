11. apakah arti kata hyper


The word 'hyper' is one of the unusual kind. It can be functioning as a prefix, which goes with other words, or as a singular adjective. The general meaning of it is 'too much'.

Penjelasan:

To find out the meaning of words, you can simply rely on any available resource now, which are fairly easy to access. Here are the meaning of that word in two different part of speech:

'hyper' as prefix = having too much of a quality. Example:

- hyperactive

- hypersensitive

- hypercritical

'hyper' as adjective = extremely nervous (anxious) or excited (exhilirated). Example:She was so hyper before the test that she constantly perspirated.He has been quite hyper lately about the vacation to Ireland this month.
