a) 2, 4, 7, 11

U6 = 2+5×3

b) 100,95,90,85 => b = -5

Un = a + (n-1) ×b

U5 = 100 + 4×-5

U5 = 100 -20 = 80

U6 = 100 + (6-1) × -5

U6 = 100 + 5 × -5

U6 = 100 -25 = 75
