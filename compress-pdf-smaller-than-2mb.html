17 x 2 = 34

17 & 34

Jawaban:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Bilangan A & B

A = 2B

A < 20

A + B = 51

51 = A + B

51 = 2B + B

51 = 3B

B = 51 ÷ 3

B = 17

A = 2B

A = 2(17)

A = 34
