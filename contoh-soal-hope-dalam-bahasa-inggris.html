1. Contoh soal pg bahasa inggris tentang congratulation, hope dan wish​


Jawaban:

Ranti: "Congratulations Salsa! you've won the short story writing competition. "

Salsa: "Thanks you Ranti. I'm really happy."

Ranti "You're welcome, I hope I can also participate in this competition next year"

Salsa: "of course,"

Ranti: "I wish actually enter this competition. "

maaf kalo salah

Jawaban:

what you say to your friend if won a competition?

a. congratulation

b. i wish

c. i hope

what did you say if your mom birthday?

a. i hope you have a long life

b. congratulation on your birthday

c. i wish you have a long life

What did you say for your best friend failed

a. i wish you success

b. i hope you success

c. congratulation

The answer:

1. a

2.c

3.b

Penjelasan:

congratulation: dipakai saat mengucapkan selamat atas keberhasilan

Wish: dipakai saat pembicaraan yang tidak kemungkinan bisa tercapai contoh i wish i can fly

Hope: dipakai saat pembicaraan yang kemungkinan bisa tercapai dan masih bisa terjadi contoh I wish I could do the test

Semoga membantu dan mohon maaf jika salah

18. Hope vs wish (perbedaan hope wish bahasa Inggris)bantu jawab dong:)​


We all hope you a speedy recovery.I hope she likes the flowers.My mother hope that you will visit us.I wish I was taller.I hope you can understand me.Don't forget, wish me luck.I wish it doesn't rain tomorrow.I hope you had finished your homework.

Perbedaan:Hope itu adalah kata yang menyatakan harapan atau keinginan yang mungkin saja dapat terjadi di dunia ini.Wish itu adalah kata yang menunjukkan harapan atau keinginan namun, harapan-harapan tersebut hampir mustahil untuk terwujud dan hanya ada sedikit kemungkinan untuk terjadi.
