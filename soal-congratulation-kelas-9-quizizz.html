4. QuizizzSoal:f(x) = 3x² - 12x - 54Tentukan:f(9) + f(6)Udah suka sama latexnya y bang? :v​


Jawab:

63

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Soal:

f(x) = 3x² - 12x - 54

Tentukan:

f(9) + f(6) =

3(9)² - 12(9) - 54 + 3(6)² - 12(6) - 54 =

3(81) - 108 - 54 + 3(36) - 72 - 54 =

243 - 108 - 54 + 108 - 72 - 54 =

63

<(7o7)>

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

f(x) = 3x² - 12x - 54

f(9) = 3(9)² - 12(9) - 54

f(9) = 3(81) - 108 - 54

f(9) = 243 - 108 - 54

f(9) = 81

f(x) = 3x² - 12x - 54

f(6) = 3(6)² - 12(6) - 54

f(6) = 3(36) - 72 - 54

f(6) = 108 - 72 - 54

f(6) = -18

f(9) + f(6)

= 81 + (-18)

= 81 - 18

= 63

22. QUIZIZZMencari volume Soal ada dilampiranpakai cara​


> Tabung <

__________

Rumus volume tabung:

[tex](\pi \times r \times r) \times t[/tex]

Dimana:

π = pi (22/7 atau 3,14)r = Jari-jarit = tinggi tabung

---

Diketahui:

r = 7 cmt = 20 cm

Maka, volumenya:

[tex](\pi \times r \times r) \times t \\ \\ = \sf(\frac{22}{7} \times 7 \: cm \times 7 \: cm) \times 20 \: cm \\ = \sf(22 \times 1 \: cm \times 7 \: cm) \times 20 \: cm \\ = \sf(22 \: cm \times 7 \: cm) \times 20 \: cm \\ = \sf 154 \: {cm}^{2} \times 20 \: cm \\ = \sf \red{3.080 \: {cm}^{3}} [/tex]

===

[tex]\huge \color{hotpi}{\underbrace{\textsf{\textbf{ \color{purple}⇣{\red{J}{\color{pink}{A}{\green{W}{\color{blue}{A}{\purple{B}{\color{gold}{A}{\orange{N}{\color{red}{}{\green}{}{\color{silver}{}{ \color{purple}⇣}}}}}}}}}}}}}}[/tex]

Diketahui

Tinggi Tabung : 20 Cm

Radius : 7 Cm

π = 22/7

Ditanya

Volumenya adalah ?

Jawaban

= Volume Tabung

= π × r² × t / π × r × r × t

= 22/7 × 7 × 7 × 20

= 22 × 7 × 20

= 154 × 20

= 3.080 Cm³
