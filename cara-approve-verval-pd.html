a. approval ⇒ penyetujuan | salah karena harus kata kerja (verb)

1. Simple Present Tense
# Nominal:
+ She is a beautiful teacher
- She is not a beautiful teacher
? Is she a beautiful teacher?
# Verbal
+ He goes to office
- He does not go to office
? Does he go to office?
2. Simple Past Tense
# Nominal
+ This test was perfect
- This test was not perfect
? was this test perfect?
# Verbal
+ She went to market
- She did not go to market
? Did she go to market?
3. Simple Future Tense
# Nominal
+ I will be smart to night
- I won't be smart to night
? will I be smart to night?
# Verbal
+ I will sleep tomorrow
- I won't sleep tomorrow
? will I sleep tomorrow?
4. Present Continous
# Nominal
+ You are being clever
- You aren't Being clever
? Are you being clever?
# Verbal
+ He is going to the bookstore
- He isn't going to the bookstore
? Is he going to the bookstore?
5. Perfect Tense
# Verbal
+ I have eaten
- I haven't eaten
? Have I eaten?
# Nominal
+ I have been smart
- I haven't been smart
? Have I been smart?

35. tuliskan contoh Bentuk nominal dan verval dalam bentuk negatif,positif dan introgative : 1.simple present tense 2.simple past tense 3.simple future tense 4.simple present continous tense 5.simple present perfect tense


1. Simple Present Tense
Nominal
(+) She is a student.
(-) She is not a student 
(?) Is she a student?
Verbal 
(+) We go to school from Monday to Saturday.
(-) We don't go to school from Monday to Saturday.
(?) Do we go to school from Monday to Saturday?

2. Simple Past Tense
Nominal
(+) My sister was very happy last night.
(-) My sister was not very happy last night.
(?) Was my sister very happy last night.?
Verbal 
(+) They went to Bali last week.
(-) They didn't go to Bali last week.
(?) Did they go to Bali last week?

3. Simple Future Tense
Nominal
(+) She will be surprised.
(-) She will not be surprised.
(?) Will she be surprised?
Verbal
(+) He will do his homework tomorrow.
(-) He won't do his homework tomorrow.
(?) Will he do his homework tomorrow?

4. Simple Present Continuos Tense
(+) Mother is cooking in the kitchen.
(-) Mother isn't cooking in the kitchen.
(?) Is mother cooking in the kitchen?

5. Simple Present Perfect Tense
(+) Viko has read the book.
(-) Viko has not read the book.
(?) Has Viko read the book?
