My Lovely Pet

i used to have a very special pet named Moch. She was a very cute cat. my friend gave it to me for my birthday present. when my friend gave her to me, she was four months old. my friend brought her back from one of his holiday trips to australia.
Moch was very beautiful, naughty, and bit aggressive too. she had white fluffy fur and purple eyes and she had a little gray spot on her head and a black spot on her tail, and she also had smell legs with very cute paws.
Moch was a very smart cat. she knew my schedule. she would wake me up in the morning, a couple of minutes before my alarm clock rang. she even knew what time i came home. when i opened the door, she would jump on me as if she was trying to tell me that she missed me so much.
Moch and i used to have great times together. unfortunately last year she died because a truck hit her. i buried her in our garden in front of my bedroom window. i really love moch.
