11. Tuliskan materi lengkap tentang WH QUESTIONS!!!​


QUESTION WORDS

A question word or an interrogative word is a word that is used to ask specific information.

These words are often called WH words because most of them start with the letters WH (ex. WHat, WHo)

Some of the most common question words in English are:

WHAT is used to ask about a spesific information about something (non-personal).

What is your name?

What does it do?

WHO is used to ask about a personal (people) subject.

Who writes the letter?

Who ate the food?

WHERE is used to ask about a place or location.

Where is Indonesia?

Where did you park your car?

WHEN is used to ask about a time or occasion.

When does the bank open?

When did you graduate high school?

WHICH is used to ask about a choice.

Which colour do you prefer?

Which dress did you buy?

WHY is used to ask about a reason or to get an explanation.

Why did she cry?

Why are you smiling?

HOW is used to ask about the way something is done (adverb) or about the condition of something/someone (adjective)

How do you bake cookies?

How is she?

How is also used to make other expressions in questions (how much, how often, how far, etc.)

Sentence formation with question words

When we use a question word in a sentence, there are three (3) sentence structures:

1. when asking about a subject

QW + verb + object/complement

ex.  

Who opens the door?

What caused the fire?

2. when asking about other parts of speech beside a subject when the verb is not a copula (be).

QW + auxiliary + subject + (object/complement)

ex.

What did he do last night?

When does the show start?

Where did you drop him off this morning?

3. When the verb is a copula (be).

QW + be + subject + (complement)

ex.

Where is he now?

What are they?

Why is she sad?

17. What is the difference between WH-Questions and yes/no questions? ​


What is the difference between WH-Question and Yes/No Question?

In Indonesian

WH-Question merupakan suatu bentuk pertanyaan yang diajukan dengan tujuan meminta informasi terkait waktu, tempat, alasan, orang, benda, keadaan, pendapat dan lain sebagainya. Contoh:

What time is it now? (Jam berapa sekarang?) Jawaban: It's nine o'clock. (Jam 9 tepat)

Sedangkan Yes/No Question merupakan suatu bentuk pertanyaan di mana jawaban yang dibutuhkan hanya "Ya/Tidak". Contoh:

Have you studied? (Sudahkah kamu belajar?)Jawaban: Yes, I have (Ya)

In English

WH-Question is a form of question asked with the aim of asking for information related to time, reason, people, object, circumstance, opinion, etc. Example:

What time is it now? (Jam berapa sekarang?)Jawaban: It's nine o'clock. (Jam 9 tepat)

Whereas Yes/No Question is a form of question where the only answer required is "Yes/No". Example:

Have you studied? (Sudahkah kamu belajar?)Jawaban: Yes, I have (Ya)

WH Questions

Who, what, where, when, whom, whose, which, why, or how

34. 1. Apakah pengertian 'WH Questions'. 2. Sebutkan rumus WH Questions dan contoh kalimatnya 2?. 3. Sebutkan 15 jenis WH Questions dan 3 contoh masing-masing.​


Jawaban:

1. WH Questions adalah sekumpulan kata tanya yang digunakan untuk menanyakan suatu hal. Contohnya waktu, tempat, orang, benda, hal, alasan, cara dan lainnya.

2. WH Questions + Auxiliary Verb (to be, do, have) + subject + main verb + question Mark (?). Contoh kalimatnya :

When will the Iron Maiden's concert start? (Kapan konser Iron Maiden dimulai?)What should I do if I got tootache again? (Apa yang harus kulakukan jika aku sakit gigi lagi?)

3. 15 Jenis WH Questions, yaitu :

1. What (Apa), digunakan untuk menanyakan informasi dari suatu hal. Contoh Kalimat :

What do you want from me? (apa yang kamu inginkan dariku?)What are you doing? (apa yang kamu lakukan?)What is your name? (siapa namamu?)

2. Where (Dimana), digunakan untuk menanyakan lokasi atau tempat. Contoh Kalimat :

Where is your home? (dimana rumahmu?)Where are you now? (dimana kamu sekarang?)Where is your parents? (dimana orang tuamu?)

3. When (Kapan), digunakan untuk menanyakan waktu. Contoh Kalimat :

When will the class ends? I'm so boring (kapan kelasnya berakhir? aku sangat bosan)When will you marry me? (kapan kamu akan menikahiku?)When does the train from andung arrive? (kapan kereta dari Bandung tiba?)

4. Why (Kenapa), digunakan untuk menanyakan alasan.
