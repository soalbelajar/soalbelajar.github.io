A. weren't going

B. don't go

C. hadn't been going

Subject + Present Tense + Subject + Future Tense

Sehingga, If you don't go, I shall be very angry

_____, I might borrow it from him.

B. The novel were his

D. If the novel him

Subject + Past Tense + Subject + might/ would/ could

Seharusnya : If the novel were his, I might borrow it from him

Penggunaan Inversi/ Pembalikan digunakan dalam kalimat tersebut,,

sehingga, Were the novel his, I might borrow it from him

26. 5 contoh kalimat future conditional sentence,present conditional sentence,and past conditional sentences


future/ type 3 :
If + subject + had + V3/past perfects + subject + would have + V3/past perfect
- If I had found her address, I would have sent her an invitation.
- If you had remembered to invite me, I would have attended your party.
- If he had asked you for forgiveness, would you have forgiven him?
- If he had called you, you could have gone.
- If I had had enough time, I would have come to your birthday party.

present/ type 1 :
If+ subject + VI (Simple Present Tense) + Subject + will + VI (Simple Present tense
- If I work hard, I will get a lot of money.
- If I have a lot of money, I will buy an expensive car.
- If I study hard, I will pass the final examination. 
- If I have free time, I will go swim at On The Rock Hotel.
- If I find her address, I’ll send her an invitation.

past/ type 2 :
If + subject + V2/simple past tense + Subject + would + Vl/past future tense
- If I were you, I would apply for that scholarship.
- If l got high a score in the final examination, my mother would give me sports car
- My husband would buy me a new car if he got a job in foreign company. 
- If I found her address, I would send her an invitation.
-  If I were you, I would not do this.

39. Contoh soal kalimat conditional sentence type 2


If my father were rich, I would live in a big villa with swimming polo inside.

(Jika ayah saya kaya, saya akan tinggal di sebuah vila besar dengan kolam renang di dalamnya.)

Keterangan: Dalam kalimat pengandaian ini, if my father was rich merupakan dependent clause yang tidak akan memiliki makna jika berdiri sendiri, sedangkan i would live in a big villa with swimming polo inside merupakan independent clause yang tetap memiliki makna meskipun tidak diikuti dengan kalimat di depan.
