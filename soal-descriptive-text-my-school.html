Descriptive paragraph is a type of text that consists of several paragraphs that describe in detail an event, object, place, and others. For descriptions, text is divided into three types, namely subjective, spatial and objective descriptions.

Characteristics of Description Paragraphs

Contains many types of adjectives.

Described in detail.

Give the reader an effect as if the object is close to them.

Describe the object, whether it is the object of a person, place, and so on.

Involve the function of the five senses in the process of describing an object.

It involves the physical characteristics detected on the object, such as color, size, shape, and so on.

Descriptive text tentang my uncle​

===========

¶ Descriptive text => teks yang isinya menjelaskan suatu benda, orang dan tempat secara khusus ( spesifik ).

¶ Generic Structure

=> Identification : Bagian pembuka paragraf yang terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dibahas

=> Description : Bagian paragraf kedua yang menjabarkan ciri khas dari orang/benda/tempat yang di bicarakan.

=> Conclusion : Bagian penutup paragraf akhir yang berisi kesimpulan dari si penulis. Bagian ini optional ( Bisa ditulis atau pun tidak )

Hola, My name is Dee. I have a lovely uncle. He is my mother's brother. My uncle is an independent man.

He lives in French alone. He always does everything by himself. He has been working in French for 6 years by now. He is a handsome man. All of his friends love him. His hobbies are swimming, playing badminton and eating all healthy foods. She loves exercising everyday.

That's the reason, he has never been sick.

I'm proud of my uncle. Every time I visit to French, he always takes me to see Eiffel Tower and another beautiful places in French .

============

KEYWORDS : DESCRIPTIVE TEXT

MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

GRADE.: 7

CODE. : 5

MATERIAL : DESCRIBING PEOPLE

4. Lembah Pelangi Waterfall

Lembah Pelangi Waterfall sounds unfamiliar for either local or foreign tourists. Lembah Pelangi Waterfall is located in Sukamaju village, Ulubelu sub district, Tanggamus district, Lampung province, Indonesia. The access to this place is quite difficult because Ulu Belu sub district is a remote area in Lampung with its hilly contours which make this tourist spot elusive.

Lembah Pelangi Waterfall has two levels where the waterfall on the second level has two branches. The height of the first level waterfall is about 100 meters, while the second level waterfall is about dozens of meters. The best enchantment of this waterfall is the rainbow which appears between the valley and the waterfall as the name suggests. The soft flowing gurgling waterfall sounds is like a chant of nature which can remove the tiredness of the long trip to go there. Under the waterfall, there are several spots of warm water which can be an interesting spot for bathing.

In this place, you will be shown a panorama of natural beauty which is very interesting for every pair of eyes seeing it. Rocky hills accompanied by leafy trees will actually soothe both your eyes.
