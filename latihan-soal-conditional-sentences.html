6. Dalam conditional sentences​


Jawaban:

1) type 1: If i find it emergency, i will help her immediately

type 2: If I found it emergency, i would help her immediately.

type 3: If I had found it emergency, i would have help her immediately

Penjelasan:

Conditional Sentence Type 1

→ It is possible and also very likely that the condition will be fulfilled.

Form: if + Simple Present, will-Future

Conditional Sentence Type 2

→ It is possible but very unlikely, that the condition will be fulfilled.

Form: if + Simple Past, Conditional I (= would + Infinitive)

Conditional Sentence Type 3

→ It is impossible that the condition will be fulfilled because it refers to the past.

Form: if + Past Perfect, Conditional II (= would + have + Past Participle)

Exceptions

Sometimes Conditional Sentences Type I, II and III can also be used with other tenses.

10. Ini soal conditional sentences ka, tolong ya ka yg bisa bantu aku:)


CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

1. The hotel front desk would offer special price room rate if you didn't ask.

2. If he doesn't have the book, I will borrow it in the school library.

3. The manager would double your current income if you could stay focus when working from home.

4. If I am rich, I will buy a new car.

5. You would make your parents sad if you didn't responsible to your life.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat andai², di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi dahulu. Conditional sentence tersusun atas if clause dan main clause/result clause. If clause menggambarkan kondisinya, dan main clause menggambarkan konsekuensinya. Apabila situasinya pasti benar, maka konsekuensinya akan terwujud.

Conditional tipe 1 [First conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Rumus :

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Conditional tipe 2 [Second conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan kecil terwujud atau diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang.

Rumus :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

19. help conditional sentences​


Conditional sentences :

1. if she were'nt always so late, she would be promoted.

fact : She is not promoted because she always late.

2. She would call him if she had his number.

fact : She doesn't have his number.

3. They would have more money if they didn't buy so many clothes.

fact : They have no money because they buy so many clothes.

4. If you hadn't been late, we wouldn't not have missed the bus.

fact : They missed the bus because they late.

5. He would have taken a taxi if he had enough money.

fact : He have no money so he wouldn't have taken a taxi.

Pembahasan

Conditional sentence yaitu kalimat majemuk yang mempunyai sebuah kondisi tentang imajinasi, pengandaian, dan suatu hal yang belum pernah terjadi. Pengandaian yang diungkapkan ini dapat saja terwujud atau tidak terwujud.

Sedangkan bentuk dari conditional sentence yaitu if-clause + main clause. if clause adalah klausa yang merupakan bagian dari kalimat pengandaian. Main clause yaitu kalimat yang berisi konsekuensi dari kondisi pengandaian yang ada di awal kalimat. Selain itu formula conditional sentence juga bisa dibalik menjadi main clause + if-clause.

Contoh kalimat :

a. If i save enough money, I can go to Bali this year.

Jika aku menabung dengan cukup, aku bisa ke Bali tahun ini.

(awal kalimat berupa if – clause yang diikuti dengan main clause)

b. My sister can have dessert if she finishes her homework.

Adikku bisa mendapatkan pencuci mulut jika ia menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumahnya.

(awal kalimat berupa main clause, dan diikuti dengan if – clause)

----------------------------

There are four types of conditional.

Conditional type 1 when the condition and result are in the future.

Structure : S + simple future IF S + present simple
